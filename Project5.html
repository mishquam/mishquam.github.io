Dog Ramp

Designing a Solution for Large Dogs With Mobility Issues

By Misha Patent

Design and Discussion

For my final project, I joined a team that decided to make a retractable ramp for large dogs with arthritis and other mobility issues. Large dogs with arthritis often cannot access elevated spaces without the assistance of a human. Smaller dogs can be lifted or carried but large dogs often weigh too much. Many people resort to ramps to allow dogs to access higher places like cars or chairs, however the quality and price vary heavily. Our groupâ€™s goal was to make an open source, easy to construct, and quality dog ramp.

During the research phase, we discovered an accordion style dog ramp that could change its angle, making it more user friendly. We hope to iterate off the accordion-like design however because we are using laser cut wood instead of metal, we will likely need additional structural reinforcement.

We came up with some rough sketches for our dog ramp, and methods we hope to use to streamline the user experience without sacrificing quality
